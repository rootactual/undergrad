My Animated GIF

Before cleaning my skateboard wheels and bearings, I wondered how the pseudo-sinewave design of the wheels would look as a GIF. I shot a 15 second video with my Android phone, then utilized Giphy to compress and convert the video to GIF format. The filter added is a bit of slight-of-hand in an attempt to hide the loop and give it a "retro" feel. The loop can still be noticed, but could be masked further with use of some form of hardware stabilization.
