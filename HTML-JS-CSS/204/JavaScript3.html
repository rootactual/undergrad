A Simple Game of Tag

Directions:

  • Use the arrow keys to move the blue ball around the screen.
  • Repeatedly pressing an arrow will increase speed (up to a limit)
  • Achieve ~80% coverage of a green ball to "catch" it and turn it red
  • Once all green balls are red, the game will end
