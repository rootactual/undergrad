Audio Sampling

This audio is ripped from a video assignement I had from Russian 102 discussing my typical day. The music track is Music: Soviet March by Shane Ivers - https://www.silvermansound.com. To detach the audio from the video file, Final Cut Pro was utilized. The second audio track, Soviet March, was added within Final Cut Pro. Voice track boosted +12dB, music track dampened -12dB and faded at .75 seconds to end. From there the audio was sampled and exported as mp3 @ 44.1 kHz. That file was then processed through Human Media's Audio Converter at 8 kHz.
